Our Story

From Bangalore to Pan-India

We started as a small team of 3 developers in Bangalore, frustrated by how expensive web development services were for local businesses. Many Indian shop owners, freelancers, and startups couldn’t afford basic websites despite needing them desperately.

That’s when we decided to build Magical Web Development – a company that delivers premium quality at prices even a small ₹5,000/month business can afford. Today, we’ve helped 200+ clients across India, from Kerala to Kashmir!

Our secret? No fancy offices – just passionate developers working remotely to keep costs low for YOU.
